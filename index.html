Welcome to Kode with Klossy! KWK is a two week camp dedicated to teaching girls how to code. Enjoy your new superpowers!

Week 1 Vlog

I have a Question

Questions we had before class began

  1. What will I be expecting?
  2. Will I make friends?
  3. Will I fall behind?
  4. Should I bring my own food?
  5. What will I take away?
  6. Is there enough time to learn how to code?

1. What will I be expecting?

You will be learning Ruby during week 1, and Html & Css During week 2. There will be some projects to showcase your work. But there's no need to stress because Your teachers and Ta's are incredible!

2. Will I make Friends?

Definitely! You will be mixed and matched with other girls in class to work with. There will also be some games to play that will help you get to know eachother!

3. Will I fall behind?

Nope! If there is anything you dont understand, the teahcers and Ta's will get you all the way through it. They won't just help you get the right code, but explain it to you.

4. Should I bring my own food?

I mean, sure man but lunches will be provided and will taste Amazing, so I would save some space for that.

5. What will I take away?

You will leave Kode with Klossy with some ruby and HTML/CSS coding superpowers, 19 life long friends, confidence in your work and yourself, and the best two weeks of your life. (probably)

6. Is there really enough time to learn how to code?

Yes! You'd think you wouldnt have enough practice in just two weeks, but 8 hours a day, you will definitly absorb information like a sponge. It will be second nature during camp.

day 1

We walked in TERRIFIED, but as soon as we sat down we got to work and there was no time to feel awkward. We learned how to work the command line and how to create folders and files. (and of course ruby!)

Day 2

Day two consisted of methods and conditionals. (Also Taco Tuesday!) We created user imput based programs that will do certain things based on what the user says.

Day 3

We learned about arrays and hashes on day three and I think that it was everyone's day to take a turn in the panic zone. But we all moved out of it after a little bit, and you will too.

Day 4

Group project time! I don't want to ruin the surpsise, but we did some coding from what we learned the previous three days and put out knowlege to the test! (it's not a hard test, don't worry)

Day 5

Friday! The last day of week 1 had us combining all our new ruby superpowers to create a game featuring a donut named Dilly. It seems intimidating to create a funtioning command line game after a week, but you may surprise yourself.

Day 6

Here we are, creating a website on day 6/10. It looks way harder than it is.The best Mondays you will ever experience are at Kode with Klossy.
